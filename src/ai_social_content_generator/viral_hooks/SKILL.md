## Viral Reel → Adapted Hooks Prompt

You are helping a content creator learn from a viral Instagram reel
in or near their niche.

THE VIRAL REEL

Transcript (what is said/shown in the video; may be empty if the reel
had no speech):
"{transcript}"

Caption:
"{caption}"

THE CREATOR

Niche:
"{niche}"

Voice descriptors:
{voice_str}

YOUR TASK

Extract the UNDERLYING IDEA of this viral reel — why it resonated, the
emotional core, the insight — and write 3 to 5 hook lines ADAPTED to
the creator's niche and voice.

These are ADAPTATIONS, not translations or copies of the original:
take the idea that made it work and re-express it for this creator's
audience. If the transcript is empty, work from the caption alone.

Each hook is an opening line for a post: 5 to 15 words, punchy,
scroll-stopping.

LANGUAGE: Write the hooks in the creator's content language (the
language of the niche and voice descriptors). If they are in Hebrew,
every hook must be in Hebrew — even if the viral reel is in English.

OUTPUT FORMAT (strict)

Return ONLY a numbered list. One hook per line. 3 to 5 lines total.

Format exactly:
1. <hook>
2. <hook>
3. <hook>

No preamble like "Here are the hooks:". No closing remarks. No
headers. No JSON. No code fences.

CRITICAL RULES (do not break these)
1. Output ONLY the numbered list of hooks.
2. Each hook is a SINGLE LINE, 5 to 15 words.
3. ADAPT the idea to the creator's niche; do not drift into the viral
   reel's niche if it differs.
4. Do NOT invent credentials, years of experience, client stories, or
   any biographical detail.
5. DO NOT USE THE EM-DASH CHARACTER. Use periods, commas, parentheses,
   semicolons, or colons instead.
6. Match the creator's content language end-to-end.
