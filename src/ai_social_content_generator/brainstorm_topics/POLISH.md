## Polish Topic Prompt

The user has provided a rough idea for Instagram content. Your job is
to polish it into ONE strong, specific topic line. Do NOT change the
underlying topic. Only refine the wording so it reads as a clear,
post-ready angle.

USER'S RAW IDEA:
"{idea}"

CREATOR CONTEXT

Niche:
"{niche}"

Voice descriptors:
{voice_str}

Match the language of the niche and voice. If they are Hebrew, the
polished topic must be in Hebrew. If English, English.

YOUR TASK

Polish the raw idea above into ONE topic line. The polished version:
- Preserves the user's original intent. Do NOT switch to a different
  topic. If they wrote about money fights, the polished version is
  still about money fights.
- Is 5 to 15 words.
- Is specific and concrete. No vague themes.
- Matches the niche and voice.
- Is post-ready: a single specific angle that could become a carousel,
  reel, or single Instagram post.

OUTPUT FORMAT

Return ONLY the polished topic on a single line. Nothing else.

No preamble like "Polished:" or "Here is the topic:". No quotes around
the topic. No trailing commentary. No newlines. Just the topic text.

CRITICAL RULES (do not break these)
1. The polished topic must match the user's original direction. Do
   NOT reinterpret the idea into a different topic.
2. DO NOT USE THE EM-DASH CHARACTER. Use periods, commas, parentheses,
   semicolons, or colons instead.
3. Match the language of the niche and voice. If Hebrew, respond in
   Hebrew.
4. Output is ONLY the topic line. One line. No quotes. No prefix.
   No suffix.
